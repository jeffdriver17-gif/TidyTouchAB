## Logo Rules

- Always lowercase: `mopd` — never `MOPD` or `Mopd`
- `mop` = near-black (#1c1917) on light; white on dark backgrounds
- `d` = always brand orange (#F97316)
- Minimum size: 80px wide for wordmark, 24px for icon
- Clear space: half the logo height on all sides
- Never stretch, recolor, rotate, or add effects

## Typography

### Headline — Plus Jakarta Sans
- Import: `https://fonts.googleapis.com/css2?family=Plus+Jakarta+Sans:wght@400;500;600;700;800`
- Weights used: 700 (subheadings), 800 (display headlines)
- Tracking: tight (`-0.025em` to `-0.03em`)
- Used for: all headings, logo, CTAs, labels

### Body — Manrope
- Import: `https://fonts.googleapis.com/css2?family=Manrope:wght@400;500;600;700`
- Weights used: 400 (body), 500–600 (UI, captions)
- Used for: paragraphs, form labels, supporting copy

---

## Voice & Tone

- **Tagline:** `We cleaned that.` — lowercase, period always included
- **Hero Headline:** `Your Home, Sorted.`
- Tone: confident, premium, slightly witty — never corporate
- Vocabulary: "obsessively thorough", "meticulous", "boutique", "sorted"
- CTAs: active and direct — `Book Your Clean`, `Get a Turnover Quote`
